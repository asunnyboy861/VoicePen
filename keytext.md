## Description
VoicePen is the privacy-first voice-to-text app that works entirely offline. Powered by on-device AI, it delivers near-human transcription accuracy without ever uploading your audio to the cloud.

WHY VOICEPEN?

Whether you are recording meetings, lectures, interviews, or personal notes, VoicePen turns spoken words into editable, searchable, and shareable text instantly. No internet required. No account needed. No data collected.

KEY FEATURES

- 100% Offline: All processing happens on your device. Your voice recordings never leave your phone. Zero data collection, zero privacy concerns.
- AI-Powered Accuracy: Built on Whisper large-v3-turbo, one of the most advanced speech recognition models available, running entirely on-device for near-human transcription quality.
- Instant Transcription: Record your voice and get text results in seconds. Watch your transcript appear as you speak.
- Smart Post-Processing: Automatic punctuation correction, filler word removal, and hallucination filtering ensure clean, readable transcripts every time.
- Export Anywhere: Share your transcripts as Plain Text, Markdown, or SRT subtitle files. Copy to clipboard or share directly to other apps.
- Audio Playback: Review your recordings with built-in audio playback. Tap any timestamp to jump to that exact moment.
- Editable Transcripts: Correct or refine your transcripts with an inline editor. Changes are saved automatically.
- iCloud Sync: Your recordings and transcripts sync across all your devices via iCloud, so you always have access.
- Search and Organize: Find any transcript instantly with full-text search. Pin important recordings for quick access.
- Multiple AI Models: Choose from tiny to large models depending on your accuracy and storage needs.
- Privacy Badge: A visible indicator reminding you that everything stays on your device.
